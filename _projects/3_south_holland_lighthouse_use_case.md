This lighthouse use case focuses on the Province of South Holland and a bounded policy workflow around the Regionaal Waterprogramma, drinkwater shortage risk, source protection, and PFAS-aware evidence support.

### Typical fit

- consultation and policy-support work on freshwater availability and source protection
- linking water scarcity, salinization, PFAS pressure, and ecological system stress
- bringing Dunea, provincial planning, KRW framing, and governance material into one evidence path
- producing challenge-ready, inspectable memos and argument skeletons

### Why this matters

The real problem is not only finding more documents. It is making drinkwater risk, water quality pressure, governance choices, and biodiversity-relevant consequences legible together. This use case is designed as an auditable lighthouse story rather than a loose collection of water-policy sources.
