NatureDesk helps turn fragmented governance material into structured, traceable draft output.

### Typical fit

- policy comparison
- briefing and memo support
- governance option mapping
- source-linked first drafts for internal decision support

### Why this matters

Teams often have the documents they need, but not the time or workflow discipline to turn them into something usable quickly. NatureDesk is built to reduce that gap without hiding uncertainty.
