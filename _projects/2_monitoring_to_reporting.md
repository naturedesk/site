NatureDesk supports the step between monitoring output and governance-ready interpretation.

### Typical fit

- indicator-aware reporting support
- structured interpretation of monitoring results
- linking evidence, assumptions, and open questions
- drafting text that remains checkable by practitioners

### Why this matters

Speed matters, but so does provenance. In reporting settings, unsupported summaries create downstream risk. NatureDesk is designed to keep the evidence chain visible.
